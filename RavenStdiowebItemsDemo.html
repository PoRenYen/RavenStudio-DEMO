1992 RUSSIAN PINK TTsKO MP EXPERIMENTAL JACKET

RARE SOVIET / RUSSIAN PINK TTsKO EXPERIMENTAL JACKET USED BY NAVAL INFANTRY,THE FABRIC WAS MADE IN SOVIET BEFORE 1991, AND THIS TYPE WAS MADE FOR EXPERIMENTAL BECAUSE THE RUSSIAN ARMED FORCES DIDN'T HAVE ENOUGH BUDGET AND FABRIC TO MAKE NEW SUIT FROM FACTORY IN EARLY 90S.
IT'S A VERY RARE TYPE AND GOOD CONDITION FOR COLLECTOR.

SIZE : 44-1 OR XS
SHOULDER TO SHOULDER : 44 CM
SLEEVES : 58 CM
CHEST : 56 CM
LENGTH : 68 CM
